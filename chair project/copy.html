    Chair

    Candid Burned

    500€ (Set of two)

    A failure. An accident. A work of art. This is an experimental edition of the Candid Chair with a charred effect on the legs and frame which underlines the natural colour and pattern of the wood grain. One of the three versions of Candid available as Note Editions, each one representing a different stage in the process of shou shugi ban.

    Candid is a compact wooden chair designed by us. Appearing light and bold at the same time, Zilio A&C’s craftsmanship and expertise were integral to realising the structural challenges of this elegant piece of seating. A versatile, materially honest chair for various environments, from restaurants to the home.

    Available from now, the Candid chair in ‘natural’, untreated Ash, curated by Note for the Editions platform.


    DIMENSIONS
    • Total height: 78.5 cm
    • Width: 40 cm
    • Depth: 45 cm
    EXECUTION
    • Untreated Natural Ash
